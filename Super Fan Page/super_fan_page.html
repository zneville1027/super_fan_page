What is Euphora?

Euphoria is an American drama series targeted to older teens. This show was created by director Sam Levinson and is currently airing episodes on HBO.

List of Characters & their Background

  1. Rue
    • Rue is the main character of the show as well as the narrator.
    • She is heavily addicted to drugs after the death of her father 2 years ago.
    • She is dating Jules and hangs out with both her and Elliot often.
    • She is often depicted as dissapointing and pushing away those around her.
  2. Maddy
    • Maddy is a confident girl who goes after what she wants but she does have her faults.
    • She is in an abusive on-again, off-again relationship with Nate Jacobs.
    • Maddy is oftenly depicted as having a strong facade to hide her true vulnerability.
    • Her goal is to find true love.
  3. Nate
    • Nate is in a toxic relationship with Maddy Perez
    • Nate is traumatized by the double life his father leads.
    • Nate often manipulates situation for his benefit.
    • He is confused about his sexuality.
  4. Cassie
    • Cassie is consistently seeking male validation.
    • Cassie's dad left her family due to his drug addiction when she was younger.
    • Cassie's mother is an alcoholic.
    • Cassie's bestfriends are Maddy, Kat, and BB.
  5. Jules
    • Jules is a trans woman.
    • She is in a relationship with Rue but is cheating on her with Elliot.
    • She struggles with commitment and guilt.
  6. Elliot
    • Elliot is a drug addict and often engages with drug use when he's with Rue.
    • He has a crush on both Rue and Jules.
  7. Cal
    • Cal is an influential man in town and the father of Nate Jacobs.
    • He has secret relations with a variety of men, with whom he records.
    • He is very cunning, manipulative, and selfish.
  8. Kat
    • Kat is a fat girl that seeks out confidence and fantasy.
    • She is dating Ethan and questions her love for him.
